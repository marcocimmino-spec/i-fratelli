Who We Are

Two brothers, born and raised by the bay

We are two young Neapolitans who grew up in this world. Our family ran a restaurant for as long as we can remember, so hospitality and good food are simply in our blood.

We've travelled the world, met people from every corner of it, and always came back to the same thought: there is no place like home. I Fratelli is our way of sharing the real Naples — the volcano, the views, and a pizza made the way our family always made it. Not a tourist factory, just two friends showing you our home.
